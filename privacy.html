Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect billing info, names, email addresses, and shipping details when you place an order. We also collect usage data (IP address, browser type) to optimize store experiences.

2. How We Use Data

Your information is solely used to process transactions, manage account profiles, dispatch orders, and communicate shipping status. We do not sell user data to advertising companies.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement secure encryption (SSL/TLS) for checkout sessions. Payment card details are never stored directly on our servers; they are securely processed via certified processors.
