Frequently Asked Questions

Find instant answers to inquiries regarding ordering, delivery, and warranty support.

What is your typical shipping timeline? +
All orders placed before 2:00 PM EST ship same-day. Domestic express shipping takes 1-3 business days, while standard global shipping takes 5-10 business days.
Can I return or exchange a product? +
Yes, we provide 30-day hassle-free returns on all products in original packaging. Return shipping labels can be processed directly via your account panel.
Do your items include structural warranty? +
Absolutely. Every Veloce item carries a 2-Year Limited Structural and Technical Warranty covering internal hardware errors or fabrication defaults.
How do I contact customer support? +
Please submit a ticket through our contact form, or email us directly at islamawan297@gmail.com. Our concierge desk is online 24/7.
