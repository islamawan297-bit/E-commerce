Crafting High-Performance Luxury

Veloce was founded in 2026 with a singular, simple mandate: design workspace and lifestyle products that balance functional excellence with clean, premium aesthetics. Inspired by the minimalism of Apple and the performance of Nike.

Our Design Philosophy

We believe that the objects surrounding you should inspire focus. Every line, corner angle, and tactile feedback of Veloce devices is refined to reduce workspace clutter and maximize daily efficiency.

By utilizing premium components (like double-shot PBT keycaps, professional-grade aluminum alloys, and top-grain cowhide leathers), we ensure our products do not just look stunning, but withstand years of heavy, continuous usage.

100% Carbon-Neutral Operations

Design shouldn't come at the cost of the environment. Veloce guarantees fully recyclable paper-based packaging and carbon-offset express deliveries across all operations globally.
